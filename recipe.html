Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Mix Ingredients
  3. Place raw dough balls onto baking pan
  4. Bake for 15 mins at 375°C
